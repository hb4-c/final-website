about us

Heidi’s restaurant is an Italian restaurant that was founded by Heidi a 70 year old Italian happy lady who moved to the Texas in 1950 from Italy to start a new life after Roberto her husband got promoted in his job. Heidi was happy starting a new life in a new country because she had everything she loved; her husband, her birds and food. At a very young age had a passion for selling as well as for making food. After a while of being in Texas she had an idea why not create her a restaurant where she could share her delicious homemade recipes.She founded Heidi’s restaurant with the help of her best friend and husband Roberto in the year 1974. Her goal was to bring a smile to every customer. To do that most dishes are made from scratch so that every bite taken is magical. .
